Before you begin

Breathing slowly — around six breaths a minute — gently shifts you toward your body's “rest and calm” state, steadying your heart's rhythm and easing tension. That settling is what calls the tide of light home.

Breathe softly and never strain. If you ever feel light-headed, return to normal breathing and rest. If you're pregnant or have a heart or breathing condition, keep the pauses short with Gentle holds below.
